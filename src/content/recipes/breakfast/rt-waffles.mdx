Makes 3 waffles.

## Ingredients

| Ingredients                              | Amount   |
| ---------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Eggs _(whites and yolks separated)_      | 2        |
| Sugar                                    | 1 tbsp   |
| Butter _(melted)_                        | 2-3 tbsp |
| Milk or Half & Half _(room temperature)_ | 1 cup    |
| Vanilla Extract                          | 1 tsp    |
| Flour                                    | 1 cup    |
| Baking Powder                            | 1 tsp    |
| Fresh Fruit _(optional)_                 | -        |

## Make It

1. Separate eggs whites from yolks; placing whites in a stand mixer bowl and yolks in a large mixing bowl
1. To egg yolk bowl:
   1. Add sugar and whip until blended
   1. Stir in butter _(melted)_, milk or half/half _(room temperature)_, and vanilla
   1. Add flour, pinch of salt, and baking powder
   1. Mix until just combined, with no visible dry flour
1. Beat egg whites with stand mixer until stiff peaks form
1. Using a fork; fold the egg whites into the mixture until combined
1. Pour into a hot waffle iron
1. Add fresh fruit (bananas, raspberries, blueberries) and syrup

_Enjoy with hot tea and good company!_
